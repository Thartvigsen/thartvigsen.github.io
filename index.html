Tom Hartvigsen

I am a postdoc at MIT as a member of the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, where I work on machine learning research with Marzyeh Ghassemi.

My interests span time series, machine learning, data mining, NLP, fairness in AI systems, and applications to understand and improve health. I did my PhD at WPI, where I was advised by Elke Rundensteiner and Xiangnan Kong, funded by a GAANN PhD Fellowship.
